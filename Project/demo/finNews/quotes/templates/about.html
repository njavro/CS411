What is FinNews?

FinNews is a personalized portfolio tracker integrating the latest financial data with relevant news articles. Begin by developing a list of stocks you are interested in. FinNews will then automatically gather the most relevant news surrounding your portfolio to help you further engage and develop your portfolio.
